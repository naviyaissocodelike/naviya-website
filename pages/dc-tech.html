DC Tech & Venture

Washington DC is the most underrated city in America. While it's known for politics, the tech and venture ecosystem here is thriving and often overlooked.

Why DC?

The intersection of policy, technology, and capital creates unique opportunities that you won't find in Silicon Valley or New York.

I'm actively supporting and building within DC's tech and venture ecosystem. Here's what I'm working on and thinking about:

  • Connecting local entrepreneurs with policy makers
  • Building bridges between traditional DC institutions and emerging tech companies
  • Advocating for policies that support innovation and entrepreneurship
