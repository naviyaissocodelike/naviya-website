About

At Tala, I lead AI and crypto product strategy—designing new ways for microentrepreneurs to access capital, coaching, and financial infrastructure. I helped launch our first AI business coach and underwriter (“Lucy”), designed lending logic from the ground up, and am now piloting crypto-backed rails to support remittances and global liquidity.

I co-founded District Angels, a DMV-based investor collective making early capital more collaborative and inclusive. We run monthly deal rooms, a venture fellowship, and back high-potential startups in vertical SaaS, govtech, and fintech.

What ties it together

A belief that capital access is too important to leave to legacy systems — new tools, new voices, and new social contracts will define the next era of inclusion.

Theses & Tendencies

  • Passionate about solutions that increase agency, support the underserved, and build bridges to tradfi systems
  • Think a lot about money as a tool to climb Maslow’s hierarchy — to survive and to self-actualize
  • Build communities wherever I live; connect people who should know each other
  • Like to be helpful when I can
  • Appreciate sci‑fi, thought experiments, and systems that don’t exist yet
