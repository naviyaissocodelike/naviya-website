Fitness & Lifestyle

I CrossFit 5-6 times per week, practice intermittent fasting, and eat vegan 90% of the time. It's a social and ethical commentary in one—a way of living that challenges conventional wisdom about health, sustainability, and personal responsibility.

The Philosophy

This isn't just about personal health—it's about questioning systems, reducing environmental impact, and living in alignment with values around animal welfare and sustainable living.

My Approach:

  • CrossFit: Functional fitness that builds real-world strength and community
  • Intermittent Fasting: Challenging conventional eating patterns and learning discipline
  • Vegan Lifestyle: Reducing environmental impact and animal suffering

This lifestyle choice is both personal and political—a way of practicing what I preach about sustainability, health, and ethical living.
