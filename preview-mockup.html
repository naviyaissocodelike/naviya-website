// 04 — About

About

At Tala, I lead AI and crypto product strategy. I've helped launch our first AI business coach and underwriter (Lucy), designed lending logic from the ground up, and now pilot crypto-backed rails for remittances and global liquidity.

I co-founded District Angels, a DMV-based investor collective making early capital more collaborative and inclusive. Monthly deal rooms, a venture fellowship, and a portfolio across vertical SaaS, govtech, and fintech.

What ties it together: a belief that capital access is too important to leave to legacy systems. New tools, new voices, and new social contracts will define the next era of inclusion.

Theses & tendencies

  • Solutions that increase agency and support the underserved
  • Money as a tool to climb Maslow's hierarchy
  • Build communities wherever I live; connect people who should know each other
  • Sci-fi, thought experiments, and systems that don't exist yet
